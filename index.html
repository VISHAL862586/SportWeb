Cricket

Cricket is one of the most popular sports in the world, enjoyed by millions of fans across different countries. It is played between two teams of eleven players on a large oval-shaped field with a central pitch. The objective of the game is to score more runs than the opposing team. Players score runs by hitting the ball with a bat and running between the wickets or by hitting boundaries. The bowling and fielding team tries to restrict runs and dismiss the batsmen. Cricket offers multiple formats, including Test matches, One Day Internationals, and T20 matches. Test cricket represents the traditional and longest form of the game. T20 cricket is a short, fast, and highly entertaining format. The sport demands a combination of physical fitness, skill, strategy, and teamwork. Football

Football is one of the most popular and widely played sports in the world, followed by millions of fans globally. It is played between two teams of eleven players on a rectangular field with a goal at each end. The main objective of the game is to score goals by kicking the ball into the opponent’s goalpost. Players use their feet, head, and body to control and pass the ball, except for the goalkeeper. The goalkeeper is the only player allowed to use hands within the penalty area. A standard football match consists of two halves, each lasting 45 minutes. Football requires speed, stamina, coordination, and teamwork. The game encourages strategic thinking and quick decision-making on the field. It is played at both professional and amateur levels across the world. Major tournaments like international leagues and world cups attract massive audiences. Football promotes discipline, unity, and sportsmanship among players and fans. Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced and exciting sport enjoyed by millions of players and fans around the world. It is played between two teams of five players on a rectangular indoor or outdoor court. The main objective is to score points by shooting the ball through the opponent’s hoop. Points vary depending on the distance of the shot, including two-point and three-point shots. Players move the ball by dribbling or passing to teammates. Each team defends its own basket while trying to score on the other end. A basketball game is divided into quarters with a fixed time limit. The sport requires agility, speed, coordination, and teamwork. Basketball also demands strong hand-eye coordination and quick decision-making. It is played at school, college, and professional levels worldwide.
